If I only had 1 hour for LinkedIn every day, this is how I'd structure it for the best possible results:

20 minutes:

Leave 10 comments in the feed (5 on familiar faces + 5 completely new ones). This keeps your profile "visible" and "visited" by multiple networks.

10 minutes:

Browse profile views, and then DM 2-3 ICPs with a friendly message. This builds an easy entryway to share your offer and convert more (ideal) clients.

30 minutes:

Publish a post, then stay and hang in the comments of the post. This builds trust and community. Plus a chance at (more) engagement on your posts.

1 hour total.

No excuses. No matter who you are. No matter what kind of "chief" role you're in. LinkedIn is a must.
